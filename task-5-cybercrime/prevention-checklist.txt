Stay Safe Online - UPI Safety Tips

- Never share your UPI PIN
- Do not accept unknown collect requests
- Always check payment details
- Do not click suspicious links
- Do not trust unknown calls
- Use official apps only
- Enable phone security
- Check bank transactions regularly

UPI Tips:
- No PIN is needed to receive money
- Verify before sending money

Report Fraud:
- cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
